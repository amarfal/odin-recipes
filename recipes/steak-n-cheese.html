#1 Steak N Cheese Recipe

Directions

Step 1

Add oil to a large non-stick skillet. Saute onions and 2 tablespoons of teriyaki sauce until the onions are soft.

Step 2

Add the sliced meat and cook until meat browns slightly, add remaining 1 tablespoon of teriyaki sauce and stir to combine.

Step 3

Place cheese on the meat and stir until melted, mixing meat, onions and cheese together.

Step 4

Scoop meat mixture onto sandwich rolls.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh steak
  • 1 large white onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade
  • 4 soft sandwich rolls
  • 1 (8 fl oz) jar cheese spread
